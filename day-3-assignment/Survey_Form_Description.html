Description
This survey is designed to assess students' understanding, perceptions, and behaviors related to hand hygiene. It evaluates their knowledge of hand hygiene practices, attitudes towards its importance in personal and public health, and the extent to which they integrate these practices into daily routines. The findings aim to identify gaps in awareness, potential misconceptions, and areas requiring targeted educational interventions to promote effective hand hygiene and prevent the spread of infections.

Survey Conducted By: Kushal Bhattarai, Ghorahi, Dang, Nepal
