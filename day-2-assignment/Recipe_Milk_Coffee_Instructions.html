Step-by-step Instructions: The sequence of steps that one needs to follow for preparing milk coffee can be listed as under:

  1. Boil some water and milk.
  2. Take a cup and keep some coffee powder into it.
  3. Take some boiled water or milk and mix it with the coffee powder.
  4. Thoroughly mix the water with coffee by stirring or using a French Press.
  5. Mix the remaining boiled water or milk wit the coffee mixture.
  6. Make the final volume as desired.
